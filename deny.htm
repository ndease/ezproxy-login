Pratt Online Consent Agreement

By logging in you understand that Pratt Institute may collect Personally Identifiable Information that has been provided by you. We sometimes use cookies to keep you signed in or store classes in your shopping cart. To manage how your information is collected online, students should contact the Registrar's Office and faculty and staff should contact Human Resources. To learn more about Pratt's privacy policy, visit www.pratt.edu/privacy.
